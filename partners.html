Strategic Operating Partners

Our Execution
Backbone.

Aevum co-invests alongside two SGX-listed Singapore operating partners who provide end-to-end execution capability — from AEI project management through to day-to-day operations and asset management. This partnership model gives our investors the benefit of proven operators whose interests are directly aligned with asset performance.

SGX: W8W

Coliwoo

Singapore's Largest Co-Living Operator
Who They Are

SGX Mainboard-listed company and Singapore's largest co-living operator by market share, with a portfolio of properties across the island serving expats, young professionals, and digital nomads.

What They Do

Coliwoo designs, fits out, and operates co-living assets from end to end — covering leasing, tenant management, community experience, and building maintenance. Their brand and platform drive high occupancy across the portfolio.

Why They Matter

Coliwoo provides Aevum's co-investment pipeline with direct access to proven AEI execution capability and occupier demand — a combination that is difficult to replicate and not available through conventional real estate channels.

Key Strengths
  • Proven AEI capability: converting commercial buildings into lifestyle-led co-living assets
  • REIT-ready asset management platform supporting the exit pathway
  • Strong occupancy rates underpinned by growing structural demand
  • Full suite of services from pre-acquisition planning through stabilisation
SGX: 41O

LHN Group

Property & Facilities Management Specialist
Who They Are

SGX Mainboard-listed property and facilities management company with an established platform across Singapore. Sister company to Coliwoo, operating with deeply integrated synergies across the real estate value chain.

What They Do

LHN Group delivers the AEI project management and facilities management for each asset — overseeing construction, renovation, regulatory compliance, and ongoing building operations at the highest standard.

Why They Matter

LHN's specialist AEI and building conversion expertise underpins the execution quality that Aevum's co-investment model depends on. Their regulatory and licensing capabilities cover co-living, serviced apartment, and hotel use classes.

Key Strengths
  • Specialist in Asset Enhancement Initiatives and full building conversion projects
  • Proven project management across complex renovation and refurbishment works
  • Cost-efficient facilities management ensuring high-quality asset upkeep
  • Regulatory and licensing expertise for residential and hospitality use classes
The Partnership Model

Integrated From Acquisition to Exit

Pre-Acquisition
Coliwoo

Deal origination, AEI feasibility assessment, occupier demand validation, and preliminary conversion planning.

AEI & Construction
LHN Group

Full project management of renovation and conversion works. Regulatory approvals, contractor oversight, and on-budget delivery.

Operations
Coliwoo

Leasing, tenant management, community experience, and day-to-day building operations delivering strong, consistent occupancy.

Asset Management
LHN Group

Ongoing facilities management, preventive maintenance, and building upkeep to maintain asset quality and valuation.

Exit
Both

REIT-ready asset management platform in place. Direct sale or roll-up to a Singapore REIT, with both partners supporting the transition.
