Strategic Operating Partners

Our Execution
Backbone.

Aevum co-invests alongside two experienced partners operating partners who provide end-to-end execution capability — from AEI project management through to day-to-day operations and asset management. This partnership model gives our investors the benefit of proven operators whose interests are directly aligned with asset performance.

The Partnership Model

Integrated From Acquisition to Exit

Pre-Acquisition

Deal origination, AEI feasibility assessment, occupier demand validation, and preliminary conversion planning.

AEI & Construction

Full project management of renovation and conversion works. Regulatory approvals, contractor oversight, and on-budget delivery.

Operations

Leasing, tenant management, community experience, and day-to-day building operations delivering strong, consistent occupancy.

Asset Management

Ongoing facilities management, preventive maintenance, and building upkeep to maintain asset quality and valuation.

Exit

REIT-ready asset management platform in place. Direct sale or roll-up to a Singapore REIT, with both partners supporting the transition.
