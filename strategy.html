Target Asset Types

Serviced Apartments

Premium short and medium-stay residences targeting business travellers and relocation tenants. Resilient demand through economic cycles.

Boutique Hotels

Deal Structure

Deal-by-Deal Co-Investment.
No Blind Pool.

Aevum does not manage a commingled blind pool fund. Instead, each investment is structured as a standalone SPV, presented to co-investors with full details before capital is required.

This means you review each asset, its projected returns, and the full underwriting before deciding whether to participate — giving you complete visibility and control.

  • Full transparency — complete underwriting presented per deal
  • No blind pool risk — you choose which deals to participate in
  • Aligned incentives — GP co-invests in every deal
  • Selective concentration — build a targeted, high-conviction portfolio
