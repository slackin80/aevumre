Who We Are

Aevum Real Estate Partners is a Singapore-incorporated real estate co-investment manager founded by Benjamin Cher and Mark Lee — two seasoned investors with a combined 50+ years of private equity, fund management, and real estate experience across Asia.

We partner with Singapore's leading real estate operators to identify, acquire, and convert under-utilised commercial buildings into high-quality living assets — co-living residences, serviced apartments, and boutique hotels.

Our approach is disciplined, transparent, and long-term. We do not manage a blind pool. Every investment is presented to our co-investors with full underwriting details before capital is committed.

Our Philosophy

How We Think About Investing

Long-Term Stewardship

We acquire assets with the intention of adding lasting value — not flipping them for short-term gain. Our investment horizon is measured in years, not quarters.

Radical Transparency

We share every deal's complete underwriting with our co-investors before capital is committed. No surprises, no fine print, no blind pools.

Aligned Incentives

The GP co-invests in every deal alongside LPs. Carry is only earned above the preferred hurdle rate. We win only when our investors win.

Operational Excellence

We partner with SGX-listed operators who are directly responsible for day-to-day asset management — ensuring professional execution at every stage.
